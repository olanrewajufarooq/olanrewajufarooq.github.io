# Microgrid Control and Energy Management

## Overview

Microgrids represent a paradigm shift in electrical power distribution, enabling integration of distributed renewable energy resources, energy storage systems, and controllable loads within localized networks. This research develops distributed control strategies for coordinating generation, storage, and consumption within microgrids, enabling reliable operation under diverse operating conditions and facilitating seamless integration with the main electrical grid.

## Applications

- Remote Communities: Microgrids enabling electricity access in isolated locations
- Industrial Parks: Coordinated energy management for multiple consumers
- University Campuses: Distributed generation and storage coordination
- Disaster Resilience: Microgrids maintaining critical services during grid outages
